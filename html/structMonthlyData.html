Detailed Description

Aggregated weather readings for one calendar month.

The three vectors are parallel: windSpeeds[i], temperatures[i], and solarRaw[i] all come from the same original WeatherRecord, preserving the pairing needed for sPCC (Pearson correlation) calculations.
