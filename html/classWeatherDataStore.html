void WeatherDataStore::Build ( const Vector< WeatherRecord > &  records)

Populate the store from a flat Vector of WeatherRecord.

Iterates once through all records. Each record is inserted into Map[year][month] (created automatically via operator[] if absent) and the year is inserted into the BST (duplicates silently ignored). Wind speed is converted from m/s to km/h during insertion.

Parameters
recordsAll loaded weather records.

Collect parallel wind/temperature/solarRaw vectors for one month across ALL loaded years (used for sPCC in option 3).
